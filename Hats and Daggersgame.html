Powered By the Apocalypse

Rather fitting to the narrative, Hats and Daggers uses Powered by the Apocalypse where you always progress the story somehow, even if you fail a roll. Using sixsided dice, you roll two dice to see where you land on a scale between 2 and 12. Depending on what you roll, you either succed, partially succeed or fail (at a six or less). But even if you fail, the story doesnt stop. Your situation merely becomes more complicated or escelate.

Safeguard mankind from Terrible Horrors

The night is full of terror, and in some casers, so is daylight! The world of Hats and Daggers is full of gruesome beasts, unspeakable horros and general unpleasant to look at creatures. To keep your focus on the task and get it done despite the overwhelming terror before you is the name of the game. As all manners of ghoulish, terrifying creaturesc crawl out of the shadows, it is the agents of Hats and Daggerss job to keep mankind safe nad for the most part: unaware of the threats that lurks in their periphery.

Conspiracies Aplenty

As a member of Hats and Daggers, your exposed to any number of strnage sects, apocalytic cults, ancient Conspiracies and mysterious forces that work to thwart you at every turn. The World of Hats and Daggers is one of figuring out who to trust. With a multitude of threats, both from within and from outside, navigate a increasingly hostile and dark universe where trust in your fellow agents is paramount yet doubt lurk in every heart.

Robots and Duels have a twofold progression track, in that you not only grow as a individual, but also a a group. As part of a Duelists Association, you climb the ranks as your bring prestige to your Association. Prestige can be lost as well as won, so unlike your personal progression which lets you master more techniques and weapon skills, your Prestige is something you need to keep in mind when you act!

Wear the hat or Wield the Dagger.

The title is not just a play on cloak anddaggers, but also the type of playbooks you can choose from. Play either as a Hat aka the investigative, academic or diplimatic types. Or as the Dagger, who provide security, hunt beasts and otherwise get int othe nitty gritty. Both type of playbook is often needed and each playbook, from the Monster Hunter to the Schoolar, are made in such a way that they can bring their own twist to things.
