A wild new fronteir: Space and Magic combined!

As mankind joins other species among the stars, they do so trough the use of a unusual resource: Magic. Magic that runs trough the galaxy like giant mystical highways between the stars.

A narrative fueled system: PTBA

Brigand galaxy uses Powered by the Apocalypse where you always progress the story somehow, even if you fail a roll. Using sixsided dice, you roll two dice to see where you land on a scale between 2 and 12. Depending on what you roll, you either succed, partially succeed or fail (at a six or less). But even if you fail, the story doesnt stop. Your situation merely becomes more complicated or escelate.

Enjoy that Brigand life!

In Brigand Galaxy, its all about being two steps ahead the trouble of your path while chasing fortune and glory. But its hard when you have to pay for ship repairs, fuel, ammo and other costly things. The Brigands life is one of adventure and sticking together as you make do. The universe is vast, and soemewhere, your big score of scores await!

A galaxy unraveling

With mankind among the stars, there are any number of systems and planets to visit. And any number of species to encounter. Or to play as for that matter. But it also a place of incredible strife. Of hardships and suffering in the wake of a galaxy wide campaign of war and death. Can you survive?

The titular robots are the Gigaknekt, ancient weapons of war that once slew titans, now turned towards your fellows in duels for glory. The standard game comes with 8 different models, each with ways to modify and customize. Each with various specialities and weapons. And outside of the giant death machines, you have a large arsenal od weapon types, each with thier own set of special attacks and skills to master.

Chase the next big score.

Brigand Galaxy is a game about making do and chasing fortune. To this end, one of its narrative engines is your groups wealth, or rather its lack thereof. Chase that next payday as you and your crew try to survive a hostile and unforgiving galaxy together.

Traverse space and engage other ships in pitched battles

You cant travel space without a ship. Consider your ship a member of your crew, becouse by the end of a game, you'll have a fully customised, personal vessel. Complete with the fuzzy dice, no doubt. Engage other ships in combat, board and claim theirs for your own or blow it up!

Customizable weapons and ships!

Brigand is all about customized content. Every weapon, ship part and piece of gears have their own move. Allowing the players any number of options!
